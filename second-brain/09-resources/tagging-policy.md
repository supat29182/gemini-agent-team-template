# 🏷️ Tagging Policy

To keep the Second Brain knowledge base organized and searchable, all AI agents must follow these tagging standards in the frontmatter of Markdown files:

### Critical Rules:

- **Use Nested Tags Only** — format: `category/subcategory`
- **Every document must have at least one `#doc/*` tag**
- **Tags must be entirely in lowercase**

### Allowed Standard Tags:

```text
# Document Type
doc/index        doc/adr         doc/changelog
doc/kb           doc/diary       doc/snapshot
doc/spec         doc/eval        doc/postmortem
doc/architecture doc/brd         doc/user-story
doc/dev-plan

# AISDLC Phase
phase/inbox      phase/design    phase/implement
phase/verify     phase/ship      phase/initiation
```
